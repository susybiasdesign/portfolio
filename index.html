Locally Real Brochure

I designed this brochure for a team of real estate agents. They approached me for a trifold, but had concerns about using paper products. I offered this five-inch square bifold as an option to save paper and money, while also standing out more than a typical trifold format. The design was based off of their website's look and feel, with a little extra flair that a printed piece allows.

Loki Intelligent Logo

Loki Intelligent is a location intelligence firm offering services in geospatial information systems. Loki's original logo used a Dagaz — an Old Norse symbol meaning "dawn" — and a rough-style font to match. While ancient ruins have little to do with the company, the Dagaz has personal meaning for the owner. With this in mind, I created a clean, modern graphic with the shape of the Dagaz and the dots-and-lines style of technology network icons. The gradient hues are reminiscent of dawn. The end result is a sleek logo that represents the company as professional and intelligent, while still holding meaning for the owner.

SF SPCA Web Banner Slides

For my first freelance project with the San Francisco SPCA, I created several banner slides for their website sfspca.org — a site that gets 22,000 visitors per week. I started with client-provided photos and copy, and provided occasional photo and wording suggestions of my own. I wrote the text and came up with the conversations concept for the bunnies slide above. Cute, huh?

SF SPCA Foster Kitten Manual

This project was a redesign of a 36-page manual for foster care providers of newborn kittens. The goal of the redesign was to make the large amount of text content easier to read by breaking sections into modular blocks and using plenty of photos.

Home Hunters Logo

I designed this logo for Home Hunters, a subsidiary of Milhousing Network. Milhousing Network is an organization of realtors dedicated to helping relocated military personnel find homes. Home Hunters is the business development lead generating team for these realtors. The group is mostly female military spouses, who tend to have difficulty establishing careers due to frequent moves. The logo is feminine, yet empowering, to represent the Home Hunters team.

Logo for RBperc Productions

I created this logo for a social media and marketing consultant specializing in the music industry. The combination of the @ symbol with the music note combines both his expertise and his target audience. The logo is hot off the presses. More branding to come!

Harris & Associates Company Handbook

This was a 40-page digital handbook outlining corporate policies and information for a 200-person construction firm. My client requested a vintage-inspired look to help make the dry content look more interesting and fun to read. I was responsible for the design and production of the cover and all of the spreads. I also sourced the photography, again keeping with the vintage theme.

Arrange That Identity

I created this logo design for a startup company that creates custom music arrangements for grade school and high school ensembles. The graphic is a play on the typical "on-demand" button, extending into a finger and a music note. The graphic can be used alone as an icon to represent the company, showing, quite literally, when you press the button, you get music. The diagonal color arrangement of the full logo gives a feeling of speed, another key feature of the company's service. Shown above are a business card example and a social media avatar.

36-Page Cocktail Book

I designed this book of cocktail recipes for a distillery client whose goal to promote their spirits by selling a recipe book that featured them. I sourced photos for the recipes, then designed spreads and created color schemes that worked with each individual image, as well as the book as a whole.
