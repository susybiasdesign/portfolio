I'm Susy Bias.

I solve problems.

Solutions with a smile.

After more than a decade collaborating with corporate marketing teams, I started my own business to focus on projects that matter to me—and to YOU! Whether you're a non-profit with a collection of design needs for a gala event, a dog walker who wants a simple website, or a small business looking for help with a branding strategy, I'm ready to help solve your problems.

My greatest joys as a designer are figuring out the solution to a puzzle and making my clients happy. I'll work with you to determine how to best reach your audience, then deliver clean, high-quality results. If you already have ideas of your own, I'm great at translating other people's thoughts into visuals. I have simple goals as a designer: to make everything beautiful and do it with a smile.

For my first freelance project with the San Francisco SPCA, I created several banner slides for their website sfspca.org — a site that gets 22,000 visitors per week.
